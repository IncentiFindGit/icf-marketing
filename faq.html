Frequently Asked
Questions

Everything you need to know about IncentiFind, Property Reports, and incentive capture.

General
What is IncentiFind?
IncentiFind is the go-to database of green building incentives. We connect commercial properties and homeowners to over $70 billion in incentives offered annually by government entities and utility companies. With $500 million in savings already identified, we simplify the entire process from search to savings.
Who is IncentiFind for?
IncentiFind serves both commercial property owners and managers (office buildings, retail, multifamily, industrial, and more) and homeowners looking to capture rebates, tax credits, and utility incentives for green home improvements.
What types of incentives does IncentiFind find?
We identify incentives across all categories, including:
  • Federal tax credits (e.g., Inflation Reduction Act programs)
  • State rebate programs
  • Local government grants
  • Utility company rebates and efficiency programs
  • Financing incentives and low-interest green loans
How much money can I save?
Commercial properties capture an average of $71,000 in incentive savings. Homeowners save an average of $1,500 per year in rebates. Your actual savings depend on your property type, location, and scope of green improvements.
Free Search
What is the Free Search?
The Free Search is a no-cost preview of the incentives available to your property. Enter your address and property type, and we'll show you an overview of the incentive programs you may qualify for. No account required to get started.
Is the Free Search really free?
Yes — completely free, no credit card required. The Free Search gives you a preview of available incentive programs. To unlock the full details, including your verified eligibility, savings estimates, action steps, and installer connections, you'll need a Property Report.
What's the difference between the Free Search and a Property Report?
The Free Search gives you a broad preview of the types of incentives available in your area. The Property Report goes much further — it verifies your eligibility for every incentive, provides a Soft Estimate of dollar savings, gives you streamlined action steps to apply, and connects you with approved installers. For commercial properties, it also automatically enrolls you in Apply Services.
Property Report
What is a Property Report?
A Property Report is a detailed digital report that gives you:
  • A Soft Estimate of average dollar savings
  • A complete verified list of all incentive programs your property qualifies for
  • Streamlined action steps to apply for each program
  • Connections to approved installers (residential) or enrollment in Apply Services (commercial)
How much does a Property Report cost?
Residential: $25 per home, active for a full year.
Commercial: $500 per property, active for a full year. Bulk pricing is available for 10 or more properties — contact us for a quote.
How long is a Property Report active?
Both residential and commercial Property Reports are active for 12 full months from the date of purchase. This ensures you always have access to current incentive information, since programs change frequently throughout the year.
How quickly can I get my Property Report?
Property Reports are generated digitally and are typically available shortly after purchase. You'll be able to access your report directly through your IncentiFind account.
Apply Services (Commercial)
What is Apply Services?
Apply Services is IncentiFind's application fulfillment service for commercial properties. When you purchase a commercial Property Report, your report automatically moves into Apply Services, where our expert team manages your entire incentive application process on your behalf.
How does the 30% success fee work?
Apply Services charges a 30% success fee on the incentive dollars we capture for you. We don't collect anything until money is in your hands. This aligns our interests with yours — we only win when you win.
Is Apply Services available for homeowners?
Apply Services is currently available for commercial properties only. Residential incentive programs are typically written so homeowners can apply themselves, and our Property Report provides all the guidance you need. We connect you directly to approved installers who can also assist with the process.
What does Apply Services actually do?
Our Apply Services team:
  • Monitors your application and answers time-sensitive requests from incentive programs
  • Provides monthly status updates and collects construction progress from you
  • Communicates directly with program managers at 500+ programs nationwide
  • Leverages our incentive expertise to maximize your capture rate
  • May reserve incentive funding in advance once a scope of work is defined
Homeowners
What kinds of home improvements qualify for incentives?
Common qualifying improvements include solar panel installation, battery storage systems, EV chargers, high-efficiency HVAC, insulation and air sealing, energy-efficient windows and doors, smart thermostats, water heaters, and more. Eligibility varies by location and program.
How long does it take to receive a rebate?
After you complete your incentive application and get approved, it takes an average of 4 to 6 weeks to receive your rebate. This timeline can vary depending on the specific incentive program and the time of year.
Do I need a contractor to apply for incentives?
Many incentive programs require you to use an approved or certified installer — especially for solar, HVAC, and efficiency upgrades. Your Property Report will identify which programs have this requirement, and we'll connect you directly to approved installers in your area.
Account & Billing
Do I need an account to run a Free Search?
No — you can run a Free Search without creating an account. However, you will need to create a free account to purchase a Property Report and access your results.
How do I purchase a Property Report?
Start by running a Free Search for your property. After seeing your preview results, you can purchase the full Property Report directly from your search results page. You'll be prompted to create an account if you don't already have one.
Can I get a refund?
Please contact our support team if you have concerns about your purchase. We're committed to making sure you get value from your Property Report.
