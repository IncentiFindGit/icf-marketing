What to prepare

To start an application through Apply Services, you'll need to provide a few basic details about your property and project. Here's what we collect:

  • Property address (street, city, state, ZIP)
  • Type of improvement or service being installed
  • Primary contact name, email, and phone number
  • Recent utility bills (electric, gas, or water)
  • Letter of Authorization for us to act on your behalf
