About Us

IncentiFind is the go-to database of green building incentives, with $500 million in savings already identified.

Each year, $70 billion in green building incentives is offered by government entities and utility companies, but over $35 billion goes unclaimed. Many properties miss out on these major savings, simply because incentives are difficult to find and capture.

IncentiFind connects more properties to green building incentives. With our proprietary database, we keep incentive information up-to-date and ready-to-use in every stage of property development — both commercial and residential.

We make incentive capture a simple process. We find all incentives a property is eligible to capture. We provide streamlined action steps and offer application fulfillment services.

With our data, 5.9 million commercial properties and 125 million homes can connect to green building incentives.

Our Mission

We are democratizing access to green building incentives. Every green building choice matters, from residential upgrades like installing solar panels to large-scale commercial initiatives like achieving portfolio-wide sustainability goals.

We are leading the industry in leveraging incentives. We are the only company solving the problem of unclaimed green building incentives with up-to-date and user-friendly data.

We are transforming the world's most polluting asset — real estate. We invite alliances and partnerships in this major undertaking.

Our Vision

We envision a thriving incentive economy, one that operates equitably for incentive consumers and efficiently for incentive suppliers. With our proprietary database, we will improve and manage the entire incentive supply chain.

Diversity Statement

Accessibility and inclusion motivate our work. We are democratizing access to green building incentives, and we value diversity as essential to fulfilling this mission. We are building a team that celebrates diversity of every kind: ethnicity, gender orientation, sex, sexual orientation, economic status, religion, and ability.

Meet our Founder

Natalie Campos Goodman brings over 15 years of experience in green architecture to her role as CEO & Founder of IncentiFind.

Natalie has been Sustainability Consultant to Fortune 1000 companies with a focus on resilience for Texas cities; she also served as Disaster Response Leader post-hurricane Harvey. She has led large oil & gas clients in implementing sustainable designs in their real estate assets, living and working around the globe. She managed the design and construction of research & robotics laboratories for Texas Medical Center. Her career started at NASA Johnson Space Center as a Programmer and Planner.

With these wide-ranging experiences in green building, Natalie is leading a dynamic team in democratizing access to green building incentives.
